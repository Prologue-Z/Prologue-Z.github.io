## 🔬 Research Focus

My research lies at the intersection of **robotics** and **machine learning**, with a focus on:

1. **Continuum Robotics** - Modeling, control, and motion planning for soft/continuum robots
2. **Reinforcement Learning** - Deep RL for robotic manipulation and locomotion
3. **Motion Planning** - Trajectory optimization in constrained environments
4. **Sim-to-Real Transfer** - Bridging the gap between simulation and real-world deployment
